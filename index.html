Changeling VR: A Narrative Mystery

Description

Changeling is a narrative VR mystery game being developed in Unreal Engine. I worked on the project in Summer 2023, during which I was part of a team of 80+ developers. I worked with a focused team of 6 developers to renovation the gameplay and level design of a portion of the game.

Role: Interaction Designer

Responsibilities:

  • Developed gameplay for the Father/Dylan's level
  • Implemented new mechanics and refined existing interactions
  • Created a standardized system for snapping interactable objects together
  • Designed level space and progression
  • Wrote extensive documentation for the use of future teams

All-Wheel Brawl

Description

All-Wheel Brawl is a local multiplayer arcade game I developed with a team of 7. Our goal was to build the game for the PlayStation 5 using a devkit owned by RIT. We prototyped the game in Unity using C# to flesh out the mechanics and system architecture, then ported the project into C++ for the PS5. I was responsible for designing gameplay mechanics and architecture for the Unity prototype, as well as assisting with sound design and mastering.

Roles: Gameplay Programmer/Designer, Sound Designer

Responsibilities:

  • Designing and implementing gameplay systems
  • Balancing parameters and abilities to support player experience
  • Mastering sound FX and background music
  • Porting Unity C# scripts to custom C++ engine
  • Implementing splitscreen multiplayer in both Unity and custom engine

DokoNekoHakai

Description

DokoNekoHakai was made during the RIT x KCG 2024 Game Jam, a jam that took place in Kyoto, Japan, as part of a study abroad program. Our team had both RIT students and Japanese KCG students, and we had 48 hours to make a game showcasing "The Fun of ...". We chose to showcase "The Fun of Finding Your Pet" by making a game where you destroy your house to find and pet your cat. I was responsible for the game music and focused on composing something that felt playful and energetic, but also a bit chaotic. You can hear the result in-game or on my BandCamp!

Role: Sound Designer

Responsibilities:

  • Design soundscape to match mood and atmosphere
  • Compose background music for title screen and gameplay
  • Design stingers for win and loss screens
  • Edit and master sound effects from raw audio

CrimeSight

CrimeSight is a team project created by myself and 4 others using Unity. We used the narrative world of Jim Butcher's The Dresden Files to prototype a 3D mystery horror game in just 5 weeks.
